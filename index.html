Production Process

From Formula Development to Export-Ready Delivery

Every private label project follows a clear production path, helping buyers control product quality, packaging details and export preparation.

01 Requirement Review: Confirm product type, target market, quantity, packaging and document needs.
02 Formula & Color Development: Develop samples, match colors and adjust formulas for selected product lines.
03 Raw Material Inspection: Check incoming materials before production starts.
04 Mixing & Stability Check: Control mixing, texture, color performance and product consistency.
05 Filling & Labeling: Fill bottles, apply private label packaging and prepare product sets.
06 QC Inspection: Inspect appearance, quantity, packaging and key product details before shipment.
07 Packing & Export Documents: Pack goods and support SDS, COA, ingredient list and export document preparation.
