    Nail Prep, Remover & Cleaner

    15ml Magic Remover Gel

    Factory Price RangeUS$0.35 - 0.90 / bottle
    Reference price by bottle, label, formula and order quantity.

    A fast brush-on solution developed to help remove UV gel polish efficiently. After the top coat is lightly filed, the remover helps the cured gel layer lift and peel in approximately 1-3 minutes. It is designed for professional nail salons, nail technicians, distributors and private label nail brands.

    Non-ToxicHEMA-FreeVeganCEMSDSOEM / ODM

    ApplicationSalon, Nail Technician, DIY
    Production Lead Time7-15 Working Days

    Key Benefits

    • Brush-on application helps simplify professional gel removal.
    • Gel layer lifts and peels without traditional foil wrapping.
    • Suitable for salon service, retail distribution and private label projects.
    • Custom formula, bottle, label, logo and retail box options available.

    Recommended Use

    1. Lightly file the gel top coat to break the sealed surface.
    2. Apply remover evenly over the cured gel, avoiding skin contact.
    3. Wait approximately 1-3 minutes for the gel layer to lift.
    4. Gently remove loosened gel and clean the nail surface.
