Answering Your Questions

Frequently Asked B2B Questions

Clear answers to streamline your private label and wholesale supply planning.

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ is 1,000 bottles per system/color range for custom labeled products. For bulk orders in raw packaging (kilograms/gallons), our MOQ starts as low as 1kg per color, allowing brand builders to test new lines with minimal upfront costs.

Are your formulas EU and US compliant?

Yes, absolutely. All our nail formulations are 100% HEMA and TPO-Free, and aligned with REACH regulations and US FDA/MoCRA standards. We supply full technical dossiers, including SDS (MSDS), CPNP declarations, and SGS certification to ensure seamless clearance at custom borders.

Can you ship to our warehouse in Dubai, USA, or the EU?

Yes. We have established logistic partners specializing in chemical and cosmetics shipping (dangerous goods) to major logistics hubs including Dubai (Jebel Ali), US West/East Coast ports, and European distribution centers. We handle both DDP, CIF, and FOB terms.

How long is the typical lead time for custom OEM orders?

For pre-existing formulas with custom labels, lead times are typically 15–20 days from label approval. For completely custom colors, viscosities, or custom-shaped bottles, the lead time is approximately 30–45 days. Standard bulk-pack supply in drums ships in 7–10 days.

How do we request a physical sample kit?

You can request a free sample kit by completing our B2B inquiry form below. Let us know which systems you are interested in (e.g., Color Gels, BIAB, Base/Top, or Hand & Foot Spa), and our sales managers will follow up to arrange express delivery to your business.
