B2B & Technical FAQ

What regulatory certifications do you provide?

We provide comprehensive SDS (Safety Data Sheets) and MSDS for all formulations. Our products are fully compliant with EU Cosmetic Regulation (EC) No 1223/2009 and the US MoCRA. We offer full technical dossiers to support your CPNP (EU) and SCPN (UK) registrations.

Do you offer Private Label (OEM/ODM) services?

Yes. We specialize in Private Label solutions including custom formula adjustment, bottle sourcing, silk-screen printing, and secondary packaging design. Our facility is GMP and ISO 22716 certified.

Can I request samples before wholesale orders?

We provide professional B2B sample kits for performance evaluation. Please contact our account managers to arrange a sample shipment tailored to your specific market requirements.

What are the MOQ and lead times?

• Ready-to-Ship: Starting from 24 units per SKU.

• Private Label: 500–1,000 units depending on complexity.

• Bulk Wholesale (1kg+): 7–14 business days lead time.

Shelf life and bulk storage stability?

Our bulk formulations are industrial-grade stabilized. When stored between 15°C and 25°C, they maintain a shelf life of 24 months. The suspension system prevents heavy settling, requiring minimal agitation before bottling.

Custom viscosities for bulk orders?

Yes. For orders exceeding 10kg per shade, we offer viscosity customization to match your bottling equipment. Full TDS (Technical Data Sheets) are provided with every industrial batch.
